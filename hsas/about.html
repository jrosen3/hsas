The Harvard Student Art Show is a visual arts exhibition held annually each spring during Harvard Arts First Weekend. The Harvard Student Art Show displays and sells the artwork of students from Harvard College and the Harvard graduate schools, providing students a rare opportunity to showcase their work publicly in a professional gallery setting. In addition to exposing Harvard University to the arts present on campus, the Harvard Student Art Show also fosters a community for the visual arts at Harvard.
